Selected Projects

Research & Impact

2025
Cooling Path

A dynamic shade-oriented pathfinding system that identifies the shadiest walking routes across Dubai. Uses real-time solar data and street-level geometry to enhance pedestrian thermal comfort in arid urban environments.

Urban Heat · Pathfinding Visit project →
2025
Re-Leaf

Unsupervised urban tree biodiversity mapping from street-level imagery using spatially-aware visual clustering. Combines BioCLIP visual embeddings with TaxaBind spatial embeddings to assess large-scale biodiversity without expert labels.

Biodiversity · CV GitHub →
2025
InfraSure

Real-time structural health monitoring using 1D convolutional neural networks. Processes raw sensor data directly to detect, localize, and assess structural damage — no pre-processing or manual feature extraction required.

Ongoing
AI for Urban Sustainability

Research on how AI transforms environmental monitoring across the UAE — from real-time air and water quality tracking to drone-based mangrove planting, AI-driven irrigation systems, and smart urban planning for cities like Abu Dhabi and Masdar City.
